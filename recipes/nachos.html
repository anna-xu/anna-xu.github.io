Baked Nachos

Ingredients

You can generally get all the ingredients at Trader Joe's if you live in USA. Peppercorn oil can be found in most Asian markets like H-Mart or 99 Ranch.
  • Tortilla Chips
  • Nonstick oil spray
  • Shredded cheese (enough to cover all the chips)
  • Ground beef (vegetarian ground 'meat' ok; can also do codfish)
  • Bell pepper
  • Half a can of corn
  • Spices: Ajika, Chile Lime, Everything but the Bagel
  • Optional: Salsa, Peppercorn Oil

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Cook the ground beef. In a nonstick pan, cook all the ground beef and season with the spices. Near the end when it's close to done, add peppercorn oil for a nice kick.
  3. Dice the bell pepper.
  4. Create the baked nachos mixture.
    5. On a baking sheet, lay aluminum foil. Spray with non-stick oil spray. Put the tortilla chips on top of the aluminum foil. Add cheese. Then, add the cooked ground beef. Finally, top it off with bell pepper and corn if desired. You can also add beans if you want.
  5. Optional: Add spices on top. Do this if you want more spices!
  6. Put the nachos in the oven for approximately 8-10 minutes. You can tell when it's done by how melted the cheese is and/or how crunchy the tortilla chips look.
Enjoy!
